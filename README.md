# 🚗 Maruti True Value Sales & Purchase Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides an interactive analysis of **Maruti True Value Sales & Purchased Vehicles**. It helps visualize income, purchased cars, purchased bikes, and customer demographics across different regions, education levels, occupations, and marital status.

The dashboard is built in **Microsoft Excel** using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, Slicers, and Dashboard Formatting.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze regional income distribution.
- Compare purchased cars and bikes across regions.
- Understand customer education and occupation profiles.
- Monitor key business KPIs.
- Enable dynamic filtering using slicers.

# 📈 Dashboard Components

## 1. Total Income Region Wise

Displays total income generated from:

- Europe
- North America
- Pacific

### Insights

- North America generated the highest income.
- Europe and Pacific contributed comparatively lower revenue.

---

## 2. Income by Education

Shows income contribution based on education level.

Categories:

- Bachelors
- Graduate Degree
- High School
- Partial College
- Partial High School

### Insights

- Bachelor's degree holders contribute the highest income.
- Partial High School customers contribute the least.

---

## 3. Income by Occupation

Displays income generated from various occupations.

Occupations include:

- Clerical
- Management
- Manual
- Professional
- Skilled Manual

### Insights

- Professionals generate the highest revenue.
- Manual workers contribute the least.

---

## 4. Cars Purchased by Region

Pie chart showing purchased cars by region.

### Distribution

- North America – 52%
- Pacific – 26%
- Europe – 22%

---

## 5. Bikes Purchased by Region

Pie chart showing purchased bikes by region.

### Distribution

- North America – 49%
- Europe – 31%
- Pacific – 20%

---

## 6. Income by Marital Status

Donut chart comparing income between:

- Married (M)
- Single (S)

### Distribution

- Married – 56%
- Single – 44%
